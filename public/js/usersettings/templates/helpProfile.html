Profile Help

The Profile area is where you can change your user settings. You can also turn your public profile on or off and edit your about area.


User Settings

The user settings area allows you to change your name, email/login, and password. You can also upload an avatar image.


Public Profile

Checking or unchecking the Public Profile Page checkbox will turn your public profile on or off. If check the box, it will automatically check the Make Data Public checkbox on the Dashboard page. This will create a public profile page for you on CrumbShare.com and populate it with your map and about description.
